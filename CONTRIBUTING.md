# Contributing to ELEANOR Platform v4.0

Welcome to contribute to the ELEANOR open-source neural engineering platform. As v4.0 introduces bidirectional capabilities and embodiment frameworks, contribution guidelines have been updated to reflect the elevated safety requirements.

## Collaboration Rules
1. All contributions must comply with the **CC BY-4.0** license. Commercial exploitation of the architecture is strictly prohibited.
2. Please clearly attribute the original author and core conceptual architect: **Chenzhuo** (GumingDilian-hub).
3. Technical improvements must maintain the core design constraints: cytoplasm-only operation, zero genomic editing, and strict adherence to the **Three Inviolable Baselines** (No Enhancement, No Weakening, No Genetic Editing).
4. **Safety Mechanism Integrity:** Do not propose modifications to the **KALTSIT AND-Gate topology**, the **Global Cortical Auto-Ban logic**, or the **Isomorphic Mapping constraints** without providing rigorous biophysical or empirical validation. 
5. **Software Boundary:** The molecular hardware (DNA sequences, modification chemistries) is open-source. The safety execution software (anomaly detection algorithms, auto-ban triggers) is closed-source. Do not attempt to reverse-engineer or fork the closed-source safety binaries.
