<h2 style="color:#1a73e8; border-left:6px solid #ea4335; padding-left:12px;">🧭 What is ELEANOR?</h2>
<p style="font-size:16px; color:#202124; line-height:1.8;">
<strong style="color:#ea4335;">ELEANOR</strong> is a next-generation precision biological neural interface system that achieves <strong style="color:#34a853;">single-axon resolution</strong> neuromodulation, sensory writing, and motor intent decoding — <em>without any permanent implant, without any genomic editing, and without a single cut to the skull.</em>
</p>
<p style="font-size:16px; color:#202124; line-height:1.8;">
Instead of rigid silicon electrodes, ELEANOR deploys <strong>self-assembling, biodegradable soft-electrode circuits</strong> via intranasal delivery. These molecular machines temporarily fuse to targeted axonal membranes, perform their therapeutic or interfacing function, and then autonomously self-destruct — leaving <strong>zero synthetic residue</strong> in the brain.
</p>
<p style="font-size:15px; color:#5f6368; line-height:1.7; border-left:4px solid #9c27b0; padding-left:14px; margin:16px 0;">
💡 <strong>Naming Source:</strong> The platform name "ELEANOR" is derived from the Arknights character Eleanor, and the KALTSIT safety mechanism is named after Arknights character Kal'tsit.
</p>

<h2 style="color:#1a73e8; border-left:6px solid #673ab7; padding-left:12px;">🧬 The Molecular Hardware Stack</h2>
<p style="font-size:16px; color:#202124; line-height:1.8;">
Every ELEANOR module, regardless of direction or application, is built from the same five-layer molecular stack:
</p>
<p style="font-size:15px; color:#202124; line-height:2.0; padding:16px; background:#f8f9fa; border-radius:8px; border:1px solid #e8eaed; margin:12px 0;">
<strong style="color:#1a73e8;">Layer 1 — Delivery:</strong> Intranasal LNPs with staggered multi-injection to prevent calcium-oscillation-induced stochastic fusion<br>
<strong style="color:#1a73e8;">Layer 2 — Interface:</strong> Fluidic soft-electrode fusing to axonal membrane, zero mechanical shear<br>
<strong style="color:#1a73e8;">Layer 3 — Wire:</strong> Polythiophene-Pyrene doped conductive DNA nanowire with triple anti-hydrolysis shielding (phosphorothioate + 2'-OMe + terminal idT capping)<br>
<strong style="color:#1a73e8;">Layer 4 — Anchor:</strong> Tau-affinity binding (AD) or SpyTag/SpyCatcher covalent lock to NF-L cytoskeleton (somatic), with <span style="font-family:monospace; background:#e8eaed; padding:2px 6px; border-radius:3px;">n⁴</span> sequence specificity<br>
<strong style="color:#1a73e8;">Layer 5 — Safety ×2:</strong> CPP enzymatic self-cleavage (seals membrane gate) + <strong style="color:#9c27b0;">KALTSIT molecular AND-Gate</strong> (instant autolysis on assembly failure)
</p>

<h2 style="color:#1a73e8; border-left:6px solid #9c27b0; padding-left:12px;">🛡️ KALTSIT: The Self-Destruct Button That Thinks</h2>
<p style="font-size:16px; color:#202124; line-height:1.8;">
Most biodegradable implants degrade on a timer. KALTSIT degrades on <strong>logic</strong>.
</p>
<p style="font-size:15px; color:#202124; line-height:1.8;">
The system is split across two strands — the <strong>Wire Chain</strong> (regions A, B, C) and the <strong>Anchor Chain</strong> (regions D, E). Regions A and D are intentionally left <em>unmodified</em> and vulnerable to DNase. Regions B and E carry full anti-hydrolysis armor.
</p>
<p style="font-size:15px; color:#202124; line-height:1.8; padding:12px; background:#f3e8fd; border-radius:6px; border-left:4px solid #9c27b0;">
<strong>✅ Perfect Assembly → Survival:</strong> A hybridizes with E (E's armor shields A). D hybridizes with B (B's armor shields D). C stays exposed for CPP cleavage. All lethal sequences are hidden. The complex lives.<br><br>
<strong>❌ Any Error → Instant Death:</strong> If only one strand arrives, or they fail to hybridize, naked A and D are immediately shredded by intracellular DNase. The entire complex falls apart in minutes. Half-built garbage cannot persist in the brain.
</p>

<h2 style="color:#1a73e8; border-left:6px solid #34a853; padding-left:12px;">🎯 Application Map</h2>
<p style="font-size:15px; color:#202124; line-height:1.8;">
Ordered by <strong>therapeutic readiness</strong> (near-term → long-term):
</p>
<ol style="font-size:15px; color:#202124; line-height:2.2; padding-left:24px;">
<li><strong style="color:#ea4335;">Alzheimer's Disease — Autophagy Induction:</strong> 40 Hz / 140 Hz entrainment via Tau-anchored electrodes directly clears p-Tau aggregates through enhanced neuronal autophagy and microglial phagocytosis modulation.</li>
<li><strong style="color:#ea4335;">Sensory Rehabilitation (Blindness / Deafness):</strong> Cortical writing of encoded auditory or visual data to replace lost peripheral input. Bandwidth strictly capped at pre-disease biological normative range.</li>
<li><strong style="color:#ea4335;">Locked-In Syndrome / ALS — Communication Restoration:</strong> Read-only motor cortex decoding for high-speed text/speech synthesis. Non-surgical, biodegradable alternative to permanent implanted BCIs.</li>
<li><strong style="color:#ea4335;">Spinal Cord Injury / Paralysis — Motor Decoding:</strong> Decoding voluntary limb intent from intact M1 to control external assistive devices (wheelchairs, environmental systems).</li>
<li><strong style="color:#ea4335;">Amputation — Closed-Loop Prosthetic Embodiment:</strong> Simultaneous motor decoding (Neural-Out) + tactile feedback writing (Neural-In) to achieve genuine cortical incorporation of a prosthetic limb into the patient's body schema.</li>
<li><strong style="color:#ea4335;">Complete Paralysis — Full Avatar Embodiment:</strong> Isomorphic humanoid robotic avatar control with full closed-loop sensorimotor feedback. Strictly human-normative kinematics. No superhuman capabilities. Longest-term target.</li>
</ol>

<h2 style="color:#1a73e8; border-left:6px solid #ea4335; padding-left:12px;">⚠️ The Three Inviolable Baselines</h2>
<p style="font-size:16px; color:#202124; line-height:1.8; padding:14px; background:#fce8e6; border-radius:6px;">
<strong style="color:#ea4335; font-size:18px;">① No Enhancement.</strong> ELEANOR restores lost function to the biological human baseline. It will never confer supra-normative sensory bandwidth (no UV vision, no infrared, no echolocation for hearing individuals) or supra-normative motor capabilities (no superhuman strength or speed in robotic avatars).<br><br>
<strong style="color:#ea4335; font-size:18px;">② No Weakening.</strong> All interfaces are strictly additive and orthogonal. ELEANOR does not suppress, overwrite, or attenuate existing healthy neural circuits. If it fails to assemble, KALTSIT ensures it vanishes without a trace.<br><br>
<strong style="color:#ea4335; font-size:18px;">③ No Genetic Editing.</strong> No viral vectors. No CRISPR. No transposons. No genomic integration. The patient's DNA is never touched. The intervention is strictly episomal and transient.
</p>
